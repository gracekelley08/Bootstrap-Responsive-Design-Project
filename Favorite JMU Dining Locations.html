Grace's Favorite Campus Food

Information, pros/cons, and opinions on JMU Dining Locations

Check it out!

Panera

JMU's Panera is my all time favorite campus dining location. I've never had something at Panera that I didn't like. Located at Duke's Dining, the central location is very convenient. They are open from 7:30am-7:00pm from Monday-Thursday, 7:30am-3:00pm on Fridays, and 9:00am-3:00pm on Saturday/Sunday.

  • Pros: They offer many tasty meal options, are open for long hours, value meals for a cheaper price, a good seating area conductive for doing homework, and reletively fast service.
  • Cons: This location gets very busy at prime lunch hours (12-2pm) and there is no option to use a Duke Deal Punch.
  • Favorite Meal: Green Goddess Caprese Melt with Passion Fruit Papaya Green Tea.

Oath Pizza

Oath pizza makes thin-crust, personal pizzas with ingredients you can personalize. They offer vegan cheese and gluten-free crust. I believe that it's the best pizza on campus. They are located in Duke's Dining and open from 11:00am-3:00pm on weekdays.

  • Pros: Oath is food sensitivity/allergy friendly, has generous portions for a punch, and has consistent quality.
  • Cons: It takes a while to get your order during busy lunch hours and they removed my favorite pizza, the "Bella," from the Duke Deal menu.
  • Favorite Meal: Whole Pepperoni Pizza with a fountain drink.

Blue Ridge Bowl

Blue Ridge Bowl is the healthiest place on campus in my opinion. They offer grain/green bowls and ramen. Blue Ridge Bowl is located in Duke's Dining an open on weekdays from 11:00am-3:00pm.

  • Pros: They have a vaired selection/an option to customize bowls and the line is usually short.
  • Cons: A bit too small of a lunch serving if you're hungry.
  • Favorite Meal: Custom Bowl including quinoa, lettuce, spring mix, roasted corn, shredded carrots, feta cheese, cucumbers, hummus, chicken, and crispy onions.

DHall

DHall is the largest dining hall on campus and has the biggest selection. The menu changes frequently and they hold themed nights like "Study Abroad Sampler" and have signature meals per day like "B-Mash Friday's." It is open on weekdays from 7:00am-8:00pm and weekends from 10:00am-8:00pm.

  • Pros: Dhall has short lines, is centrally located, and is an all-you-can-eat location.
  • Cons: Can get very busy during perspective student tours and important campus events.
  • Favorite Meal: Stir fry and peanut butter pie.

The Den

The Den is located in Dukes Dining and is open on Monday-Thursday from 11:00am-12:00am and Friday from 11:00am-9:00pm. The restaurant is a version of Denny's and has many options.

  • Pros: The Den has varied options for both food and drink, is open late, and has quick food service.
  • Cons: They have limited Duke Deal options and long lines during peak lunch and dinner hours.
  • Favorite Meal: The Guacamole Chicken burrito with sweet potato fries and a fountain drink.

EHall

Ehall is the large dining hall on East Campus. They have themed nights like "A Night in Paris." Ehall is open on weekdays from 7:00am-8:00pm and weekends from 10:00am-8:00pm.

  • Pros: There are very short lines, plenty of open seats, they offer diverse options, and is an all-you-can-eat location.
  • Cons: Ehall is far from main campus, where many classes and events are held. There are also fewer options when compared to DHall.
  • Favorite Meal: Chicken burrito bowl with tortilla chips and homemade ice cream.
